# Blended AI: Better Together

Blended AI combines different AI techniques—such as neural networks, expert systems, and statistical models—to create more robust solutions.

## Benefits
- Improved accuracy
- Greater adaptability
- Reduced bias

This approach mirrors how diverse teams outperform uniform ones.
