# AI for Creativity: Why Human Touch Still Matters

While AI can generate art, code, and music, human creativity brings emotional depth, cultural context, and originality.

## The Human Edge
- Emotional intelligence
- Cultural nuance
- Innovative risk-taking

AI should be seen as a collaborator, not a replacement.
