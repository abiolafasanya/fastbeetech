# The 6 AI Trends You'll See More of in 2025

AI adoption is accelerating. Expect to see:
1. Autonomous AI agents
2. AI in creative industries
3. AI-enhanced healthcare
4. Multimodal AI
5. AI-driven automation
6. AI for sustainability

These trends will define the next wave of digital transformation.
